[04] QUALS: QUantum Algorithmic Ledger System

QUALS is any federated tokening and exchange system that uses time-entangled Qubit Coin distributed ledgers, which are cryptographically verified via seals.

QUALS Forks:

  • Interstellar Block Investment System: IBIS
    This is the core block and is the standard implementation of QUALS for interstellar trade. Seal dates for pDAOs, corporations, governments, and non-governmental organizations all exist on the IBIS public ledger. Contains optional zero-knowledge systems.

    This is a fork of IBIS and is the primary QUALS used for currency and market exchange.
